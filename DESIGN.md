# DesignHutch — Design System & UX Direction

## Visual Direction

Dark sleek futuristic with premium motion.

Think:
- Black / charcoal backgrounds
- Strong contrast
- Clean spacing
- Smooth transitions
- Sharp typography
- Strategic bursts of colour

---

## Colour Palette

### Base
- #050505 (near black)
- #111111
- #1A1A1A

### Text
- #FFFFFF
- #BDBDBD

### Accent Options
Use sparingly:
- Electric Blue #3B82F6
- Purple #8B5CF6
- Orange #F97316

Primary recommendation:
Blue + Orange combo

---

## Typography

### Headings
Bold geometric sans-serif:
- Sora
- Space Grotesk
- Inter Tight

### Body
- Inter
- Manrope

---

## Logo Direction

Icon + wordmark

Concept:
A modern mark inspired by:
- Hutch / structure
- Motion / growth
- Crafted digital space

Wordmark clean and modern.

---

## Layout Principles

- Large spacing
- Bold hero statement
- Section rhythm
- Cards with subtle glow
- Grid layouts
- Strong visual hierarchy
- Minimal but rich

---

## Motion Principles

Smooth premium motion only.

Use:
- Fade + slide reveals
- Hover elevation
- Subtle parallax
- Animated gradients
- Soft CTA interactions

Avoid:
- Gimmicky over-animation
- Slow heavy effects
