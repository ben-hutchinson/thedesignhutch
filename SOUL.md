# DesignHutch — Brand Soul Document

## Core Identity

DesignHutch is modern craftsmanship for local businesses.

It blends premium digital design with honest personal service.

Not a faceless agency.
Not a restrictive template platform.
A real expert building real business growth.

---

## Mission

Help small businesses enter the modern age with websites that look premium, perform properly, and stay affordable.

---

## Vision

Become a respected founder-led studio generating recurring side-income and trusted by local businesses.

---

## Brand Archetypes

### Primary
The Creator
- Builds beautiful things
- Values craft
- Solves through design

### Secondary
The Guide
- Honest
- Supportive
- Trustworthy
- Helps non-technical owners succeed

---

## Emotional Outcomes

When visitors arrive they should feel:

- This looks premium
- This person knows what they’re doing
- This feels trustworthy
- This is achievable for my business
- I want to enquire

---

## Personality Traits

- Modern
- Trustworthy
- Personal
- Sharp
- Helpful
- Confident
- Clear

---

## Tone of Voice

### Should Sound Like:
- Expert but human
- Clear not jargon-heavy
- Friendly confidence
- Practical
- Modern and energised

### Avoid:
- Corporate fluff
- Buzzwords
- Pushy sales language
- Overly technical explanations
