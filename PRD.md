# DesignHutch — Product Requirements Document

## 1. Overview

DesignHutch is a founder-led web design studio focused on helping local businesses modernise their online presence with premium websites, lower running costs, and personal support.

The website’s primary objective is to convert visitors into qualified leads through:

- Free consultation bookings
- Direct enquiries
- Strong first-impression credibility

---

## 2. Core Goals

### Business Goals

- Generate consultation bookings
- Establish trust quickly
- Showcase capability through design quality
- Position DesignHutch as a better alternative to DIY website builders
- Create a scalable lead engine for local outreach

### User Goals

Visitors want to know:

- Can this person help my business?
- Will it look professional?
- Can I afford it?
- Is this easier than doing it myself?
- Can I trust them?

---

## 3. Target Audience

### Primary Users

Local independent businesses with storefronts:

- Garages
- Bike shops
- Tailors
- Record shops
- Retail stores
- Service businesses
- Appointment-based businesses

### Secondary Users

Growing businesses needing:

- E-commerce
- Automation
- Booking systems
- Website redesigns

---

## 4. Value Proposition

Premium websites for local businesses without agency pricing, platform limitations, or impersonal service.

### Core Hooks

- Free design consultation
- No payment until design approved
- Ongoing support available
- Built by a professional developer
- Affordable premium quality

---

## 5. Site Architecture

## Pages / Sections

### Single Page Structure

1. Hero
2. Services
3. Portfolio
4. Process
5. FAQ
6. About
7. Contact CTA

Optional future pages:

- Case Studies
- Pricing Guide
- Blog / SEO Content
- Booking Page

---

## 6. Functional Requirements

### Hero Section

Must include:

- Strong headline
- Clear value proposition
- CTA buttons:
  - Book Free Consultation
  - Send Enquiry

### Services Section

Cards for:

- Brochure Websites
- E-commerce Stores
- Booking Systems
- Hosting Help
- Automation / AI Tools

### Portfolio

Grid of showcased projects:

- Personal projects
- Professional examples
- Expandable case studies later

### Process

Simple 3–4 step flow:

1. Free Consultation
2. Design Approval
3. Build & Launch
4. Ongoing Support

### FAQ

Must answer:

- How much does it cost?
- How long does it take?
- Do you offer support?
- Can you redesign my current site?
- Can you help with hosting?

### About

Founder-led positioning:

- Professional developer
- 5 years coding experience
- Personal service
- Accountable communication

### Contact

Methods:

- Form
- Email
- Calendly

---

## 7. UX Requirements

- Fast loading
- Mobile first
- Smooth animations
- Clear CTAs throughout
- Trust within first 5 seconds
- Easy enquiry flow
- Premium feel without clutter

---

## 8. Non Functional Requirements

- SEO ready
- Responsive on all devices
- Lighthouse optimized
- Accessible contrast / semantics
- Secure contact handling
- Easy future content updates

- Bounce rate under 50%
